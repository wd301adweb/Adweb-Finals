Common Symptoms of COVID-19

Most common symptoms: fever, dry cough, tiredness Less common symptoms:, aches and pains, sore throat, diarrhoea, conjunctivitis, headache, loss of taste or smell, a rash on skin, or discolouration of fingers or toes and Serious symptoms: difficulty breathing or shortness of breath, chest pain or pressure, loss of speech or movement

Aches and Pains

Minor pains and discomforts, typically on your body and the muscles.

Sore Throat

Pain or Irritation of throat that often worsens when you swallow.

Headache

Pain, tightness, or pressure around your forehead or the back head and neck.

Loss of taste or smell

Causes of impaired taste range from the common cold to more serious medical conditions.

Rash on skin

Many rashes are itchy, red, painful, and irritated. Some rashes can also lead to blisters.

Difficulty breathing

Tightening of chest, air hunger, difficulty breathing, and feeling of suffocation.

Dry cough

A dry cough is a cough that doesn't bring up mucus, or tickle in the back of your throat.

Fever

Having a temperature above the normal range due to an increase in the body's temperature.
